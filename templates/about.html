ABOUT DISCIPLINE

Over the years, the name of Joint Effort Tutorials has become synonymous with success in CBSE Examinations. We have over the years designed, delivered, perfected and innovated the art and science of teaching and guiding students for CBSE board exams and entrance exams.

Our goal at J.E. Tutorials is to provide knowledge and guidance and thereby create an environment that not only guides students to the path of success, but also inspires them to recognize and explore their own potential. J.E. Tutorials is run by a group of professional academecians, who have over the years, guided thousands of aspirants to fulfill their dreams.

All Courses are based on the time tested teaching methodology which has been perfected by J.E. Tutorials and which has produced unmatched results in CBSE consistently over the past 20 years.
